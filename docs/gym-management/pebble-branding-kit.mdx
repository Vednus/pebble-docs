## Pebble Branding

Download the zipped file below to get the Pebble logo and other branding assets. You can use these to let your members know that your gym is using the Pebble platform.

[PebbleLogosSignage.zip](https://pebble-branding.s3.amazonaws.com/PebbleLogosSignage.zip)

Assets included in the zip file:

- Pebble single color logos in black and white (SVG)
- Pebble logo (png and svg)
- Pebble logo and name (png and svg)
- Pebble horizontal logo and name (svg)
- Two pebble posters (pdfs)
